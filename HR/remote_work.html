Remote Work Guidelines

At Boeing, we recognize the benefits of remote work and are committed to providing the support and resources needed to ensure successful and productive work-from-home experiences. These guidelines are designed to help you maintain efficiency and communication while working remotely.

1. Eligibility for Remote Work

Remote work eligibility is determined based on the nature of the job role and the employee's performance. Consider the following:

  • Job roles that allow for independent work are generally eligible.
  • Employees must meet performance expectations consistently.
  • Approval from a direct supervisor and HR is required.

2. Home Office Setup

To ensure productivity and comfort, follow these home office setup recommendations:

  • Choose a quiet, well-lit space dedicated to work.
  • Ensure ergonomic furniture and equipment are used.
  • Maintain a strong and stable internet connection.
  • Utilize company-provided hardware and software.

3. Communication and Collaboration

Effective communication is crucial for remote work success. Adhere to the following:

  • Use company-approved communication tools like email, Slack, or Microsoft Teams.
  • Attend scheduled virtual meetings punctually.
  • Keep your calendar updated and accessible to team members.
  • Regularly update supervisors on progress and challenges.

4. Work Hours and Availability

Maintain a structured work schedule to balance work and personal life:

  • Stick to standard working hours unless otherwise agreed upon.
  • Be available during core business hours for meetings and communication.
  • Inform your team of any deviations from your regular schedule.
  • Take regular breaks to avoid burnout.

5. Data Security and Privacy

Ensure the security and confidentiality of company data while working remotely:

  • Use secure connections and VPNs when accessing company networks.
  • Keep devices and software updated to prevent security breaches.
  • Adhere to company data protection and privacy policies.
  • Report any security incidents immediately to the IT department.
