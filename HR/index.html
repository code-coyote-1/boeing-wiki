Employee Wellness Programs

At Boeing, we believe that the well-being of our employees is essential for a productive and fulfilling work environment. Our wellness programs are designed to support physical, mental, and emotional health, and encourage a healthy work-life balance.

1. Fitness Programs

Our fitness programs are designed to keep employees active and healthy:

  • Onsite Gym: Access to state-of-the-art gym facilities.
  • Fitness Classes: Yoga, Pilates, Zumba, and more.
  • Virtual Workouts: Online classes for remote employees.
  • Reimbursement: Subsidies for gym memberships outside the office.

2. Mental Health Support

We prioritize mental health with comprehensive support services:

  • Employee Assistance Program (EAP): Confidential counseling and support.
  • Workshops: Stress management and mindfulness sessions.
  • Online Resources: Access to mental health apps and tools.
  • Hotline: 24/7 support for mental health crises.

3. Nutrition and Healthy Eating

We promote healthy eating habits through various programs:

  • Nutritional Counseling: Access to dietitians and nutritionists.
  • Healthy Cafeteria Options: Balanced meals and snacks.
  • Workshops: Nutrition education sessions.
  • Cooking Classes: Learn to prepare healthy meals at home.

4. Financial Wellness

We support financial health through educational resources:

  • Financial Planning: Workshops and one-on-one sessions with financial advisors.
  • Retirement Planning: Information sessions about retirement savings.
  • Debt Management: Resources to help manage and reduce debt.
  • Investing Basics: Learn about investing and wealth management.
