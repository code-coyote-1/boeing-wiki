Employee Code of Conduct

Welcome to the Boeing Employee Code of Conduct page. Our company is committed to maintaining a work environment where ethics and integrity guide all our actions and decisions. This code is a guide to the standards of behavior expected from all employees.

1. Integrity and Honesty

Employees are expected to act with integrity and honesty in all business dealings. This includes:

  • Providing accurate and truthful information.
  • Avoiding conflicts of interest and disclosing any potential conflicts.
  • Maintaining confidentiality and respecting privacy.

2. Respectful Workplace

We are committed to creating a workplace where all employees are treated with respect and dignity:

  • Harassment, discrimination, and bullying are strictly prohibited.
  • Promote inclusivity and diversity.
  • Encourage open communication and teamwork.

3. Compliance with Laws and Regulations

Employees must comply with all applicable laws and regulations:

  • Adhere to industry standards and legal requirements.
  • Report any illegal or unethical conduct.
  • Understand and comply with company policies.

4. Responsible Use of Company Resources

Employees should use company resources responsibly and efficiently:

  • Avoid misuse of company equipment and facilities.
  • Protect company information and assets.
  • Use technology and resources for business purposes only.

5. Reporting Violations

Employees are encouraged to report any violations of this Code of Conduct:

  • Reports should be made to a supervisor or HR representative.
