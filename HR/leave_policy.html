Leave Policies

At Boeing, we understand the importance of work-life balance and are committed to providing our employees with the necessary support to manage their personal and professional lives. Below are our leave policies, outlining the various types of leave available to you.

1. Annual Leave

Employees are entitled to annual leave to rest and recharge:

  • Eligibility: All full-time employees after six months of service.
  • Leave Duration: 20 days per year, pro-rated for part-time employees.
  • Procedure: Submit a leave request at least two weeks in advance.

2. Sick Leave

Sick leave is available for employees who are unwell and unable to perform their duties:

  • Eligibility: All employees, from the start date.
  • Leave Duration: Up to 10 days per year.
  • Procedure: Notify your supervisor as soon as possible and provide a medical certificate if the leave exceeds three days.

3. Parental Leave

We offer parental leave to support new parents:

  • Eligibility: Employees with at least 12 months of service.
  • Leave Duration: Up to 12 weeks of paid leave for primary caregivers.
  • Procedure: Notify HR at least eight weeks before the expected start date.

4. Bereavement Leave

Bereavement leave is provided for the loss of an immediate family member:

  • Eligibility: All employees.
  • Leave Duration: Up to 5 days per occurrence.
  • Procedure: Inform your supervisor and HR as soon as possible.

5. Unpaid Leave

Unpaid leave may be granted for personal reasons:

  • Eligibility: All employees.
  • Leave Duration: Subject to managerial approval.
  • Procedure: Submit a detailed request to your manager outlining the reasons and duration.

For more information, please refer to the Boeeing Employee Handbook or contact the HR department.
