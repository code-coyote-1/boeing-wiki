Health Insurance Plans

Boeing is committed to providing comprehensive health insurance options to support the well-being of our employees and their families. Below are the details of the health insurance plans offered, including coverage, eligibility, and additional resources.

1. Health Maintenance Organization (HMO) Plan

The HMO plan provides comprehensive health services through a network of providers. Key features include:

  • Eligibility: All full-time employees.
  • Coverage: Primary care, specialist visits, hospital care, and prescription drugs.
  • Network: Requires choosing a primary care physician (PCP) and referrals for specialists.
  • Cost: Lower premiums with co-pays for services.

2. Preferred Provider Organization (PPO) Plan

The PPO plan offers flexibility and a wide network of healthcare providers. Key features include:

  • Eligibility: All employees and eligible dependents.
  • Coverage: In-network and out-of-network services, hospital care, and prescription drugs.
  • Network: No need for PCP selection or specialist referrals.
  • Cost: Higher premiums with lower co-pays and deductibles for in-network services.

3. High Deductible Health Plan (HDHP) with Health Savings Account (HSA)

The HDHP provides lower premiums and is paired with an HSA for tax-advantaged savings. Key features include:

  • Eligibility: All employees.
  • Coverage: Preventive care, hospital care, and prescription drugs.
  • HSA: Allows pre-tax contributions to cover eligible medical expenses.
  • Cost: Lower premiums with higher deductibles.

4. Dental and Vision Plans

Boeeing also offers separate dental and vision coverage to ensure comprehensive health support:

  • Dental Plan: Covers preventive care, basic and major services, and orthodontics.
  • Vision Plan: Covers eye exams, glasses, and contact lenses.
