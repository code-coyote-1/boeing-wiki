Retirement Savings Options

Boeing is dedicated to helping employees plan for a secure and prosperous retirement. We offer several retirement savings options to meet the diverse needs of our workforce. Below are the details of the retirement savings plans available to you, including benefits, eligibility, and resources.

1. 401(k) Plan

The 401(k) plan is a tax-advantaged savings plan that allows employees to save for retirement through pre-tax contributions. Key features include:

  • Eligibility: All employees aged 21 and over.
  • Contributions: Employees can contribute up to the IRS limit, with Boeeing matching up to 5% of your salary.
  • Vesting: Company match vests over a 3-year period.
  • Investment Options: A variety of investment funds are available to choose from.

2. Roth 401(k) Plan

The Roth 401(k) plan offers after-tax contributions, allowing for tax-free withdrawals in retirement. Key features include:

  • Eligibility: All employees aged 21 and over.
  • Contributions: Employees can contribute up to the IRS limit.
  • Tax Benefits: Contributions are made with after-tax dollars, and withdrawals are tax-free in retirement.
  • Investment Options: A range of investment funds to tailor your portfolio.

3. Pension Plan

Boeeing's pension plan provides a guaranteed income stream for eligible employees in retirement. Key features include:

  • Eligibility: Full-time employees with at least 5 years of service.
  • Benefits: Defined benefit based on salary and years of service.
  • Vesting: Fully vested after 5 years of service.
  • Payout Options: Choose from various payout options, including lump-sum and annuity payments.

4. Employee Stock Purchase Plan (ESPP)

The ESPP allows employees to purchase Boeeing stock at a discounted rate. Key features include:

  • Eligibility: All full-time employees.
  • Discount: Purchase Boeeing stock at a 10% discount.
  • Contribution Limits: Up to 15% of your salary, subject to IRS limits.
  • Enrollment: Open enrollment periods twice a year.
