Employee Assistance Programs

Boeing is committed to supporting the well-being of our employees through our comprehensive Employee Assistance Programs (EAP). These programs offer a range of services to help you manage life's challenges and improve your quality of life.

1. Mental Health Counseling

Our mental health support services provide confidential counseling and resources:

  • Individual Counseling: Access to licensed therapists for personal or work-related issues.
  • Group Therapy: Support groups for stress, anxiety, and other mental health concerns.
  • Online Resources: Access to self-help tools and mental health apps.
  • 24/7 Hotline: Immediate support for mental health crises.

2. Financial Advice

We offer financial planning and advice to help manage your finances:

  • Budgeting Assistance: Tips and tools for managing your finances effectively.
  • Debt Management: Strategies to reduce and manage personal debt.
  • Retirement Planning: Guidance on saving for retirement and investment options.
  • Financial Webinars: Educational sessions on various financial topics.

3. Legal Assistance

Our legal assistance services provide support for various legal matters:

  • Legal Consultations: Free or discounted consultations with attorneys.
  • Document Preparation: Assistance with wills, contracts, and other legal documents.
  • Legal Resources: Access to online legal libraries and tools.
  • Identity Theft Prevention: Resources to protect against identity theft.

4. Work-Life Balance

We provide resources to help you achieve a healthy work-life balance:

  • Flexible Work Arrangements: Options for remote work and flexible scheduling.
  • Parenting Support: Resources and tips for working parents.
  • Time Management Workshops: Techniques for balancing work and personal life.
  • Wellness Programs: Programs to support physical and mental well-being.
