New Employee Checklist

This checklist is designed to help you navigate your first days and weeks at Boeeing. It covers everything from paperwork to meeting your team, ensuring you have all the information and resources you need to start successfully.

1. Pre-Start Preparation

  • Complete and return your employment contract and other required documents.
  • Review the welcome email from HR for important information about your first day.
  • Set up your Boeing email account and familiarize yourself with company tools.
  • Read the Boeing Employee Handbook to understand company policies.

2. First Day at Work

  • Attend the New Employee Orientation session.
  • Meet with your manager for a welcome meeting and discuss your role and expectations.
  • Set up your workstation and install necessary software and tools.
  • Familiarize yourself with the office layout and facilities.

3. First Week

  • Complete required training modules in the Training Portal.
  • Set up meetings with your team members and key stakeholders.
  • Review your role-specific tasks and responsibilities with your manager.
  • Understand the company's communication tools like Microsoft Teams and Outlook.

4. First Month

  • Participate in team meetings and contribute to ongoing projects.
  • Schedule a feedback session with your manager to discuss your progress and goals.
  • Explore the Employee Assistance Program (EAP) for additional support services.
  • Engage in company events and activities to get to know your colleagues.

5. Resources and Support

Here are some resources to assist you during your onboarding process:

  • Access the IT Support Portal for technical assistance.
  • Contact HR for any queries regarding benefits and policies.
  • Use the Wellness Programs to maintain a healthy work-life balance.
  • Review the FAQs for common onboarding questions.
