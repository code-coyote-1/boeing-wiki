Company History

Founded in 1916, Boeing has been at the forefront of aviation and aerospace innovation. Our journey has been marked by groundbreaking achievements and a commitment to excellence in every aspect of our business. Below is a timeline highlighting key milestones in our company's history.

  • 1916

    Boeing is Founded

    William E. Boeing establishes the company, initially focused on designing and manufacturing aircraft.

  • 1933

    Introduction of the Boeing 247

    The Boeing 247, the first all-metal airliner, revolutionizes commercial aviation.

  • 1958

    Launch of the Boeing 707

    The Boeing 707 becomes the first successful commercial jet airliner, ushering in the Jet Age.

  • 1967

    Introduction of the Boeing 737

    The Boeing 737 is launched, becoming the best-selling commercial jetliner in history.

  • 1995

    Entry into Space Exploration

    Boeeing expands into space exploration, playing a key role in the development of the International Space Station.

  • 2011

    Launch of the Boeing 787 Dreamliner

    The Boeing 787 Dreamliner, featuring advanced technology and fuel efficiency, takes flight.

  • 2020

    Commitment to Sustainability

    Boeeing commits to sustainability with initiatives focused on reducing environmental impact and advancing green technology.
