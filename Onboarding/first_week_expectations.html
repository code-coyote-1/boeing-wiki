First Week Expectations

As a new member of the Boeing family, your first week is all about getting acclimated, meeting your colleagues, and understanding your role. This guide provides a detailed overview of what to expect during your initial days with us. Follow this guide to ensure a successful start at Boeeing.

Day 1: Orientation and Introduction

Schedule:

9:00 AM - 10:30 AM: Welcome and Company Orientation

Location: Conference Room A

11:00 AM - 12:00 PM: Team Introductions

Location: Department Office

1:00 PM - 2:30 PM: HR Policies and Benefits Overview

Location: HR Office

3:00 PM - 4:00 PM: Office Tour

Location: Meet in the Lobby

  • Complete your employee paperwork and submit it to HR.
  • Set up your workspace and computer access.
  • Attend the orientation session to learn about Boeeing's mission and values.
  • Introduce yourself to your team and key stakeholders.

Day 2: Training and Setup

Schedule:

9:00 AM - 10:00 AM: IT Setup and Access Training

Location: IT Department

10:30 AM - 12:00 PM: Safety and Compliance Training

Location: Safety Office

1:00 PM - 3:00 PM: Department-Specific Training

Location: Your Department Office

3:30 PM - 4:30 PM: Q&A with Your Manager

Location: Manager's Office

  • Set up your IT credentials and access all necessary software and tools.
  • Attend safety and compliance training to understand workplace protocols.
  • Participate in department-specific training sessions to learn about your role.
  • Have a one-on-one meeting with your manager to discuss expectations.

Day 3: Getting to Know the Team

Schedule:

9:00 AM - 10:00 AM: Team Coffee Meet and Greet

Location: Break Room

10:30 AM - 12:00 PM: Project Overview Session

Location: Project Office

1:00 PM - 3:00 PM: Collaboration Workshop

Location: Conference Room B

3:30 PM - 4:30 PM: Open Office Hours with Team Lead

Location: Team Lead's Office

  • Join a coffee meet and greet to socialize with your new colleagues.
  • Learn about ongoing projects and your contributions to them.
  • Participate in a collaboration workshop to enhance teamwork skills.
  • Visit your team lead during open office hours for additional guidance.

Day 4: Work and Responsibilities

Schedule:

9:00 AM - 11:00 AM: Work Assignment and Task Breakdown

Location: Team Office

11:30 AM - 12:30 PM: Lunch with Mentor

Location: Cafeteria

1:00 PM - 3:00 PM: Hands-On Work Session

Location: Your Workspace

3:30 PM - 4:30 PM: Team Feedback Session

Location: Meeting Room C

  • Receive your work assignments and understand your daily responsibilities.
  • Have lunch with your assigned mentor to gain insights and advice.
  • Engage in a hands-on work session to start contributing to projects.
  • Attend a team feedback session to discuss progress and challenges.

Day 5: Review and Reflection

Schedule:

9:00 AM - 10:00 AM: Self-Reflection and Goal Setting

Location: Your Workspace

10:30 AM - 11:30 AM: Review Meeting with Manager

Location: Manager's Office

1:00 PM - 3:00 PM: Final Training Session

Location: Training Room

3:30 PM - 4:30 PM: Team Wrap-Up and Closing

Location: Conference Room D

  • Take time to reflect on your first week and set personal goals.
  • Meet with your manager to review your progress and expectations.
  • Attend any remaining training sessions to complete your onboarding.
  • Join a team wrap-up meeting to share experiences and insights.
