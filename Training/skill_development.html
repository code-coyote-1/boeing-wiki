Registration Information

To register for a workshop, follow these steps:

  • Visit the Workshop Registration Portal.
  • Select the workshop you are interested in and click "Register."
  • Fill out the registration form with your details.
  • Submit the form and wait for a confirmation email with further instructions.

Please note that spaces are limited, and registrations are on a first-come, first-served basis.
